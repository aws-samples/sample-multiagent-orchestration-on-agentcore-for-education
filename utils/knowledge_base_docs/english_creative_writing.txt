English - Creative Writing

Creative writing expresses imagination and personal voice through fiction, poetry, drama, and creative nonfiction. Fiction writing develops characters, plots, settings, and themes through narrative techniques. Poetry explores language, rhythm, imagery, and emotional expression. Dramatic writing creates dialogue, conflict, and character interaction. Creative nonfiction combines factual content with literary techniques. Writing processes include brainstorming, drafting, workshopping, and revision. Voice, style, and perspective distinguish individual writers. Publishing opportunities include literary magazines, contests, and digital platforms. Creative writing develops imagination, empathy, and communication skills.
