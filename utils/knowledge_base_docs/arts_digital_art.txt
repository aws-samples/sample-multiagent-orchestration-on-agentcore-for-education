Arts - Digital Art and New Media

Digital art uses computer technology as a creative medium, expanding artistic possibilities through software, hardware, and internet platforms. Digital painting and drawing use stylus and tablet interfaces. 3D modeling creates virtual sculptures and environments. Animation brings static images to life through motion. Interactive art responds to viewer participation through sensors and programming. Net art exists primarily online, exploring internet culture and connectivity. Virtual and augmented reality create immersive experiences. Digital art challenges traditional concepts of originality, authenticity, and permanence while offering new forms of creative expression and distribution.
