Arts - Art History and Movements

Art history traces artistic development through major movements and cultural periods. Ancient art includes Egyptian, Greek, and Roman traditions establishing classical principles. Medieval art features religious themes in Byzantine, Romanesque, and Gothic styles. Renaissance art emphasizes humanism, perspective, and naturalism. Baroque art displays drama, movement, and emotional intensity. Modern movements include Impressionism, Cubism, Surrealism, and Abstract Expressionism. Contemporary art explores conceptual ideas, multimedia, and social commentary. Art reflects cultural values, historical events, and technological innovations while influencing society and individual expression.
